Grandma's Banana Bread

Description

Grandma's homemade banana bread. This recipe can be used to make banana bread, banana muffins, or even zucchini bread with some adjustments!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 cup oil
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 3 bananas, very ripe

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Mix together eggs, sugar, vanilla, and oil in a large bowl.
  3. Combine flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix.
  5. Mash bananas and add to batter.
  6. Spray two loaf pans with non-stick spray, and divide batter between them. (Alternatively, use four mini loaf pans.)
  7. Bake for about 1 hour (40 mins for mini loaves.)
