Baked Oatmeal

Description

Homemade baked oatmeal, just like mom used to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil or butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar (can substitute 1/2 splenda
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups oats
  • 3/4 tablespoon (=2 1/4 teaspoon) baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (opt.)
  • 1 1/4 cups milk

Steps

  1. Mix oil, sugar, and egg.
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Pour butter into greased 9"x9" pan, and bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees (or until firm & golden brown).
